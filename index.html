What is hosting?

Website hosting refers to the service of providing storage space and server resources for websites, making them accessible on the internet. It allows businesses to publish their websites online, ensuring they are available to visitors 24/7.

Do you provide website hosting?

We offer website hosting through established partnerships. Our ‘Peace of Mind’ coverage provides essential services without hosting, allowing you to keep your existing hosting setup. For those seeking optimized performance, our ‘Peace of Mind + Hosting Help’ package includes affordable, managed hosting designed to speed up your site.

Do I have to commit for a year?

No, you don’t have to commit for a year! The  subscription is monthly, not annual.

We ask for one months notice to wind down coverage.

What search engine optimisation (SEO) do you do?

We ensure that all sites meet a basic SEO standard and maintain it as effectively as possible.

In cases where additional improvements are needed, we provide expert advice.

What updates do you take care of?

We handle all necessary updates for your site, covering both server-related tasks and site-specific maintenance.

These updates are crucial for maintaining security and preventing hacking incidents.

Can you add Google Analytics?

We can add Google Analytics as a part of every site onboarding.

Adding Google Analytics to your website is a great idea. It provides valuable insights into your site’s performance and user behavior. You’ll be able to track visitor information, traffic sources, user behavior, conversion rates, and more.

Can I call you in an emergency?

Absolutely. We are here to be a technical point of contact when you need it
